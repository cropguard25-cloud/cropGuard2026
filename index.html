CropGuard is an early-stage agritech solution designed to reduce bird-related crop losses using intelligent detection and adaptive deterrence — built for practical, real-world farming conditions.

Learn more

The Problem

Birds cause significant crop damage for small and mid-scale farmers. Traditional scare tactics are predictable and lose effectiveness, while solutions like netting or laser systems are often costly, labor-intensive, or inaccessible.

Our Approach

  • Detection of real bird activity
  • Adaptive and unpredictable deterrence
  • Designed to reduce bird habituation
  • Affordable, solar-friendly, and offline-capable

Why CropGuard Matters

Food Security

Reducing avoidable crop loss helps protect farmer livelihoods and local food supply.

Farmer-First Design

Built for environments with limited infrastructure, connectivity, and capital.

Adaptive by Design

Focuses on changing bird behavior over time, not just short-term scaring.

CropGuard is currently in its early development stage. The project is focused on research, prototyping, and field-relevant design.
