About CropGuard

CropGuard is an early-stage agritech initiative focused on reducing crop losses caused by bird damage through adaptive and practical deterrence methods.

Why CropGuard Exists

Many farmers experience repeated crop losses due to birds, yet available solutions are often ineffective, expensive, or impractical for everyday farming conditions.

CropGuard was conceived to explore a more adaptive approach — one that responds to real bird activity and reduces habituation over time, while remaining affordable and suitable for low-resource environments.

Vision

CropGuard aims to support farmers by reducing preventable crop damage, improving food security, and encouraging the development of agricultural technologies that are grounded in real-world conditions.

CropGuard is currently under development and focused on research, prototyping, and concept validation.
